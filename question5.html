When looking at numbers, we naturally associate them to numerical value's. This question require's you to look at the numbers a bit differently.
Look at the appearance of each number and the appearance of the letters that make them up.
The letter A: Only made up from straight lines (like 1's, 4's, and 7's)
The letter B: Formed from straight and curved lines (like 5's and 12's)
The letter C: Formed only from curved lines (like 0).
What shape is the letter 16 made from?

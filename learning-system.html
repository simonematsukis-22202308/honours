Simulated Learning System Quiz


This section will contain a quiz that mimics a learning system (for example, Moodle Quizzes or Learn ICE tasks).

This quiz contains 8 questions. You are required to answer all of them.
Please read the information below before beginning the quiz.

Information:
  1. Once you have submitted your answer, you cannot go back to the previous answer. Please make sure you are happy with your selected answer before proceeding to the next question.
  2. All 8 questions are multiple choice. You will also have access to hints and resources related to the question if you need assistance. A hint will directly assist you in answering the question whereas resources will give you background information that will guide how you answer the question.
  3. There is no time limit to complete this quiz, but as soon as you start it you will be timed. Each individual question will also be timed.
  4. After completion, you will recieve your results, as well as your identified learning style.
